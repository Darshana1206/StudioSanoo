If you are looking for a Delaware wedding photographer or Philadelphia wedding photographer, you’ve come to the right place! With award winning wedding photography and videography services available, let Barnyard Photography capture your special day!

Our Style


Our style of wedding photography is a mix of traditional, lifestyle and photojournalism. This means we are always looking for honest and candid moments but step in and provide direction and styling when needed. For more information regarding our wedding photography and videography services and packages.

For portrait sessions we strive to provide a fun, relaxed environment for our clients and help them “fall into a pose.” It is the most important thing we can do to make your portraits look great.

During ceremonies and formalities, our number one goal is to remain unobtrusive while capturing great moments and not distract you and your guests.

Our aesthetic is classic and uncomplicated. We try to avoid fads and aim to provide you with timeless photos that you will love for years to come.
